A bot made to let you choose

Bismarck is an open-source bot made with the Rust
programming language and can adapt to all hosting options!

Features

A math expression evaluator

A mathemathical expressions parser to evaluete
your expressions for all of you math nerds out there!

A genshin impact wishes simulator

A simulator to Genshin Impact's wishes, specifcally for
genshin's lovers to have fun!

A simple to use UI

The bot provides a simple and intiutive interface to make it
easier are more convinient to use!

Management for servers

The bot also provides efficient tools for you to manage
your discord servers, helping you maintain a fun and happy community!

Ready to get started?

A whole lot of fun is waiting for you, to enjoy it!
